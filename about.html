About Me

My Journey

I'm James Burke, documenting my 90-Day Pivot Challenge at 50+. After decades in the printing industry as a professional and business owner, I'm learning new skills and sharing what I discover along the way.

Why This Site

This website captures my progress, ideas, and the free tools and services I discover. My goal is to share what I learn so others can benefit too - especially those who might be considering their own pivot or just want to understand AI better.

What I'm Learning

My focus is on AI tools and how to use them - particularly free options that don't require budgets or subscriptions. I'm exploring:

  • OpenCode and AI coding assistants
  • Free AI models via OpenRouter
  • Modern terminal tools like Ghostty
  • Free website hosting options
