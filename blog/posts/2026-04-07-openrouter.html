OpenRouter.ai: Accessing Free AI Models

Categories: AI, Free Services

OpenRouter.ai is an API that aggregates multiple AI models into one service. The key feature? Many models are completely free - no credit card required.

What OpenRouter Offers

  • Unified API: One integration for dozens of models
  • Free tier: 28+ free models available
  • No signup cost: No credit card needed
  • Routing: Automatic selection based on your needs

How Free Models Work

OpenRouter subsidizes free models to promote accessibility. You get access without paying:

  • openrouter/free: Router that auto-selects the best free model
  • Multiple providers: NVIDIA, Qwen, Meta, and others
  • Generous limits: Enough for learning and experimentation

Popular Free Models

  • Qwen 3.6 Plus: 1M context, excellent for coding
  • Nemotron 3 Super: Open weights from NVIDIA
  • Llama 3.3 70B: Meta's multilingual model

Why It Matters

For someone learning AI tools, OpenRouter provides access without cost barriers. You can:

  • Try different models to find what works
  • Learn prompt engineering for free
  • Build projects without budgeting for AI
  • Experiment freely

My Setup

I'm using OpenRouter with OpenCode to get free AI assistance in my terminal. It means I can use AI tools without worrying about costs while I'm learning.

The free models aren't the most powerful, but they're capable enough for learning and building personal projects.
